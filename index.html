Welcome to Point Park Club Tennis

Point Park Club Tennis is a student organization at Point Park University in Pittsburgh, PA. We are a group of students who love to play tennis and have fun. We welcome players of all skill levels and are always looking for new members. If you are interested in joining, please contact us or come to one of our events.
